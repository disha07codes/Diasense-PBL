Emergency & Help

Immediate actions and support for critical situations

Low Blood Sugar (Hypoglycemia)

Common Symptoms: Sweating, shaking, dizziness, confusion

  • Consume fast-acting sugar (glucose tablet, fruit juice)
  • Rest and recheck sugar after 15 minutes
  • Seek medical help if symptoms persist

High Blood Sugar (Hyperglycemia)

Common Symptoms: Headache, dry mouth, fatigue, nausea

  • Drink water to stay hydrated
  • Avoid sugary foods
  • Consult a doctor if levels remain high
